Welcome To Donate A Meal

This website is for donating amount so that we can help someone who need food.

The world is living through unprecedented times. The coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic is causing disruption & uncertainty in regular wage/salaried employees in urban India. These daily wage workers facing uncertain challenges in meeting their daily food requirements. As is often the case, people living in poverty are suffering the most.
